Tier definitions
Jade — $7,000
Top chapter tier. Includes event tickets, IAG tickets, up to 15 memberships, and branding. Current Jade sponsors: JPMC ($7K), Robert Half & Protiviti ($7K). PwC is $4K Jade.
Silver — $5,000
Strong chapter partner. Includes 5–10 event tickets, 8–10 IAG tickets, option to host one event, branding. Current: RSM, Crowe, Sargent & Lundy, CIBC, Encova.
Bronze — $3,000
Core partner. Includes 5–8 event tickets, IAG access, branding. Current: Hyatt, Nationwide, Mars Wrigley, Wipfli, Riveron.
Supporter — $1–2.5K
Mission-aligned contribution. Variable benefits. Current: Ingredion ($2.5K), KraftHeinz ($1.5K), West Monroe ($1K).

Engagement tier definitions
Tier 1 — High
Active Ascend member at this company AND attended 2+ events. Warmest touchpoint possible. Action: open sponsor conversation now — they already believe in the value.
Tier 2 — Warm
Active member OR 2+ event attendees (not both). Real connection exists. Action: leverage the active member as an internal champion, or invite attendees to a dedicated conversation.
Tier 3 — Contact
A named contact exists (from Sponsor Targets doc) but limited or no member/event data. Action: follow up with that contact directly before the relationship goes cold.
Tier 4 — Cold
No active member, no event attendees, no named contact. Action: LinkedIn research first — find the ERG lead or DEI Director before any outreach.
