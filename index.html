Tier definitions
Gold — $10,000
Top-tier partner. Includes IAG naming rights, executive speaking opportunity, maximum event tickets, maximum memberships, full branding across all channels.
Jade — $4,000–$7,000
Mid-premium partner. Includes event tickets, IAG tickets, memberships (up to 15), and branding. Current Jade sponsors: JPMC ($7K), Robert Half & Protiviti ($7K), PwC ($4K).
Silver — $5,000
Strong chapter partner. Includes 5–10 event tickets, 8–10 IAG tickets, option to host one event, branding. Current: RSM, Crowe, Sargent & Lundy, CIBC, Encova.
Bronze — $3,000
Core partner. Includes 5–8 event tickets, IAG access, branding. Current: Hyatt, Nationwide, Mars Wrigley, Wipfli, Riveron.
Supporter — $1,000–$2,500
Mission-aligned contribution. Variable benefits. Current: Ingredion, KraftHeinz, West Monroe.

Engagement tier definitions
Tier 1 — High
Company has at least one active Ascend member AND attended 2+ events. Warmest possible touchpoint. Action: open sponsor conversation now.
Tier 2 — Warm
Company has an active member OR 2+ event attendees, but not both. Action: leverage the member as internal champion for intro.
Tier 3 — Contact
No active member data and 0–1 event attendees, but a known contact exists (from Sponsor Targets doc). Action: follow up with known contact before relationship goes cold.
Tier 4 — Cold
No known connection — no member, no attendees, no named contact. Action: LinkedIn research to find ERG/DEI lead before any outreach.
